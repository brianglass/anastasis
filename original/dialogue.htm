THE DIALOGUE OF THE ANAPHORA

In the translation of the Divine Liturgy, issued with the blessing of His All-holiness the Oecumenical Patriarch and Archbishop Gregorios of Thyateira and Great Britain, the priest's invitation, commonly translated, ‘Let us lift up our hearts‘, is translated, ‘Let our hearts be on high‘.
